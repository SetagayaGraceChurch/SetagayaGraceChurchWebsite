Create a clean, welcoming homepage design for a Christian church in Setagaya, Tokyo, Japan.

Audience:
Japanese people who may not be familiar with Christianity and may be visiting a church for the first time.

Design style:
Simple, calm, and welcoming.
Avoid flashy or overly emotional styles.
Use soft neutral colors such as white, light gray, soft blue, and warm beige.
Typography should feel clear and trustworthy.

Layout structure:

Hero section
Large welcoming photo of a small church gathering
Headline: 世田谷にあるキリスト教会です
Subheadline: 聖書を学び、人生の希望について考えることができる温かい教会です
Two buttons: 礼拝のご案内 / 初めての方へ

Service information section
Title: 日曜日の礼拝
Display service times clearly:
子どもクラス 14:00–14:45
礼拝 15:00–16:30
Location: 世田谷区羽根木
Include a map placeholder.

What happens at church section
Three columns explaining:
賛美（歌）
聖書のお話
お祈り
Include reassuring text saying first-time visitors are welcome.

About the church section
Photo of people talking together after church.
Short description of the church community.

Pastor introduction section
Portrait of the pastor with a short introduction.

Christianity explanation section
Title: キリスト教とは？
Brief explanation of Christianity for people unfamiliar with it.

Sermons section
List of recent messages with play buttons.

Final invitation section
Friendly welcome message encouraging visitors to come.
Buttons: 礼拝のご案内 / お問い合わせ

Navigation menu:
Home
初めての方へ
礼拝のご案内
教会について
メッセージ
お問い合わせ

Design should look modern but simple, appropriate for a church in Japan.
